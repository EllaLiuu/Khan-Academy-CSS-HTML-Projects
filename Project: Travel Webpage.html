About Vietnam

Vietnam is truely a beautiful place and everyone should visit it at least once in their lifetime. With it's amazing landscapes, complex history, great food and fascinating energy, Vietnam will really capture your heart. Vietnam is exoctic and wild but at the same time it is very relaxing. The lifestyle over there is very different and truely unique which makes it an even better experience. Not only are the people there extremly welcoming and kind but there are many gifts from nature aswell. Due to Vietnam's warm climat, it's often considered a tropical place and there are many special fruits that you can find only in Vietnam!

History and Background Information

Vietnam is a country located in the South East Asia. It has a estimated population of 94.6 million people which makes Vietnam quite a densily populated place considering that it is only 331,231 km2. The capital of Vietnam is Hanoi but the largest city is actually Ho Chi Minh City. Vietnam has a very complex and long hisotry which would take a long time to explain. In short, Vietnam has had many wars in the past since many other countries have tried and succeed in colonizing them such as China, France and America.

Things to know before going to Vietnam

It is important to be prepared before you travel and this will help you have a safe and scam-free trip! Firstly, sort out your VISA and all those important documents before you go since you don't want to be stuck at the airport! Secondly, the weather varies alot so you should always be prepared for the weather, whether it's raining or a sunny day. Lastly, Vietnam is a great place for foodies as there are so many unique foods there! So, keep your stomachs empty before you go!
